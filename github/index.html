Design the quilt.
We do the math.

PatchWorx is a browser-based design tool for quilters. Piece blocks from squares, half-square triangles, rectangles, and flying geese. Lay them out with sashing and borders. The second you're done, PatchWorx hands you a print-ready PDF pattern, complete with cutting list, yardage, and step-by-step methods. Works in any browser. Works on iPad. No software to install.

Free for beta testers · Expect a few rough edges · We're listening

What's in the box

Design like a quilter. Publish like a pro.

PatchWorx walks the whole path with you, from the first pieced unit to a print-ready, sewable pattern, with the fabric math handled the whole way.

Block

Piece blocks from the shapes you actually use.

Squares, half-square triangles, rectangles, flying geese. Drop them on a grid, rotate, mirror, recolor. Save a block once and reuse it everywhere. Construction diagrams included.

Quilt

Lay out the whole top, sashing and all.

Tile your blocks across any grid size. Alternate plain and pieced, rotate them into pinwheels, drop in sashing, cornerstones, and borders. See the finished quilt the second you place a block.

Pattern PDF

A print-ready pattern, ready to publish.

When the design is done, PatchWorx hands you a finished PDF: accurate cut sizes, fabric yardage, and a step-by-step methods section.

Why PatchWorx

A canvas that thinks like a quilter.

Fabric math is the tedious part. PatchWorx does it the moment your block is finished, in the browser, free, with no $240 install standing in the way.
